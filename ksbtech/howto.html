How To Sign Up On Cardtonic

Click the 'Register" button. The registration form will popup which include the following field:

1. Full name

2. Email(Kindly ensure your email address is correct as this is where updates concerning your transactions will be sent)

3. Password (it is advisable to use a password that ONLY YOU can figure out).

4. Mobile Number

How To Verify Your Email Address

Once registered, there's need to verify your email address before you can use your account. This is a very simple process. Once registration is submitted, check your email address for the verification message from Cardtonic. The message is titled "Welcome to Cardtonic - Verify Email". If you did not find this mail in your inbox, kindly check the spam folder

Once your email address is verified, you can proceed to log in to your account

How To Sell Gifts Cards

- Click on the "Start A Trade" button on the app homepage

- Select the brand of card you want to sell in "Category"

- The "Subcategory" option is for you to be more specific with your type of card. For instance, the amazon gift card brand has several options, there's Amazon gift card with cash receipt, there's Amazon gift card with debit receipt and amazon with no receipt. The subcategory is where you specify which option describes your gift card.

- Enter the total amount of the gift card you want to trade.

- The total payable amount will be displayed according to the current rate.

- Attach your gift card pictures and receipt(if trade requires receipt), you can upload up to 20 pictures at a time.
