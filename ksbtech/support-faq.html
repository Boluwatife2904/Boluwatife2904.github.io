General Gift Card Questions
  1. What Is The Difference Between eCode, Physical Cards And Printable Vouchers

    2. Physical gift cards are actual physical (plastic) cards that are purchased directly from a physical store. Physical gift cards are safest and will fetch you the best money return.

    Ecodes are digital gift card codes purchased online and mostly sent to the buyer’s email address. Ecodes are not as safe as physical gift cards thereby the money value is always lower.

    On the other hand, printable vouchers also known as paper codes are somewhat in between physical gift cards and ecodes. It is similar to an e-gift code because only code is purchased and also similar to physical gift cards because they can be printed physically like a receipt.

  2. What Is The Similarity Between eCode, Physical Cards And Printable Vouchers

    3. They are similar because they work the same way as they are all valid gift cards of respective providers.

    But they differ in money return and safety, for more information on this, check their differences also.

  3. What Is Face Value Or No Face Value Gift Card

    4. This is a card whose real amount is printed on it and as such the real value can be ascertained prior to the trade.

    For instance, if you see the amount of a card written on it then it has face value, otherwise, it has no face value.
